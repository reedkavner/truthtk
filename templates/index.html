TruthTK is a bot that donates to ProPublica whenever @realDonaldTrump tweets "fake news."

${{ total }} donated since Feb 12, 2017

Why?

Trump's relationship with the truth is troubling and his efforts to discredit legitimate journalists are chilling. Supporting ProPublica's work is a small way to fight back.

How much does it donate?

This is the fun part!

  • $1 for any tweet containing "fake news"
  • + $1 if "FAKE NEWS" is in all caps
  • + $1 if he mentions either @cnn or @nytimes
  • + $1 if he incorrectly calls @nytimes "failing"
  • + $5 if he mentions @propublica (which he never has)
For example: The failing @nytimes does major FAKE NEWS China story saying "Mr.Xi has not spoken to Mr. Trump since Nov.14." We spoke at length yesterday! is good for $4.

What is ProPublica?

They're a Pulitzer-winning nonprofit investigative newsroom. Learn more about them and their work at propublica.org. The bot is not affiliated with them.

How can I participate?

This project is open source! Grab the code from GitHub to run your own TruthTK bot.

You can also donate to ProPublica the regular way.

What's up with the name?

"TK" is newsroom speak for "to come." With a President who has a habit of making up his own facts, a well-supported independent press is our best hope for pushing the conversation towards truth.
